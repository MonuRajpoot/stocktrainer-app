Introduction

This Privacy Policy outlines how Kalkaji collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our website and services. We're committed to being transparent about our data practices and giving you control over your information.

Last updated: August 13, 2025

Information We Collect

To provide you with the best learning experience, we collect the following information:

  • Your name, email, and phone number during registration
  • Payment details when you purchase a course (processed securely)
  • Course progress and quiz results to track your learning journey
  • Device information and IP address for security purposes
  • Cookies and usage data to improve our platform
How We Use Your Data

Your data helps us create a personalized and effective learning experience:

  • Provide personalized course recommendations and learning paths
  • Track your progress and provide meaningful insights
  • Send important course updates and platform notifications
  • Improve our services based on usage patterns
  • Prevent fraud and ensure platform security
  • Provide customer support when you need help
Data Security

We take your data security seriously and implement multiple layers of protection:

  • Industry-standard SSL encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure servers with regular security updates and monitoring
  • Two-factor authentication options for your account
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Limited access to your data on a need-to-know basis
  • Automatic logout after periods of inactivity
Sharing of Information

Your trust is important to us. Here's our commitment regarding data sharing:

  • We never sell, rent, or share your personal data for marketing purposes
  • We only share data with trusted service providers (like payment processors)
  • We may share anonymized, aggregated data for research and improvement
  • We comply with legal requirements when required by law
  • We will notify you of any significant changes to our sharing practices
Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience:

  • Essential cookies for platform functionality and security
  • Analytics cookies to understand how you use our platform
  • Preference cookies to remember your settings and choices
  • You can control cookie settings through your browser
  • Some features may not work properly if cookies are disabled
Your Rights & Control

You have full control over your personal information:

  • Access and download all your stored data
  • Correct or update your information at any time
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Port your data to another service
  • File a complaint with relevant data protection authorities

Questions About Your Privacy?

We're here to help! Contact our privacy team for any questions or concerns.
