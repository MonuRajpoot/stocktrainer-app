📖 What is Fundamental Analysis?

Fundamental Analysis is like checking the health of a company before buying a part of it. Just like you wouldn't buy a second-hand car without checking its engine, mileage, and service history — you shouldn't invest in a company without analyzing its financials, business model, and growth potential.

🔍 Key Questions to Ask:

  • ✓ Is this a strong and trustworthy company?
  • ✓ Is it making profits consistently?
  • ✓ Will this company grow over the next 5–10 years?
  • ✓ Is the stock overvalued or undervalued?

🍕 Pizza Shop Analogy

Your friend Rohan wants ₹5 lakhs for his pizza shop expansion. He offers you 10% ownership for ₹50,000. What would you check?

📊 Is his shop profitable?
💳 Does he have existing debts?
🍕 How many pizzas sold daily?
👥 Are customers returning?
🏪 Expansion plans?
